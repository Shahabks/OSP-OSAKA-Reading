We are Transforming Data into Knowledge

We've got a small group of scientists, artists, engineers, and educators who have one thing in common "curiosity" .

We are excited about extracting information from observed data, particularly unstructured datasets.

We architect machine algorithms to transform data into knowledge. We bring together technologies in computational, cognitive, and behavioral sciences to better understand human development processes.

Novel speech processing algorithms

What we do

At Mysolution lab, we carry out research, do conceptual design, engineer software and transfer knowledge by teaching and publishing papers journals.

1- research

our research focuses are:

  • * information processing of acoustic signals of spoken languages,
  • * dynamics of the collective intelligence life-cycle.
  • * innovative methods for Human-Machine Interface.

and some of the research topics we are excited about:

  • * natural language processing and acoustic modelling,
  • * computer speech and logic,
  • * human-computer interaction,
  • * human-computer learning,
  • * human-computer naturalistic speech communications,
  • * information and knowledge visualization, art,
  • * complex system behaviour, brain.
